# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Trust Assertion Drift Monitor is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- compare board, investor, buyer, and partner trust language against actual proof freshness and contradiction load
- identify where assertion confidence is outpacing the evidence room or owner continuity
- convert drift into one usable remediation plan with clear close, refresh, restate, or escalate decisions

This is an operator-facing trust-intelligence surface, not legal advice or a live compliance system.
